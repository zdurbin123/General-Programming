Together, we can create a change in people's lives

 

We work in the hardest-to-reach places, where it’s toughest to be a Vulnerable.

The scale of crisis in the world has increased exponentially in recent years. There are more people living in conflict and war zones now than at any time in the past 20 years, as well as more refugees. Natural disasters are not only more frequent, but more severe. As always, children in crisis are among the most vulnerable. In particular, the more than 12 million refugees now face a triple jeopardy. Not only have they lost their homes and lost their education, they now face the additional impact of COVID-19 on their futures.

Charity Most Urgently In Need
Health

Hunger

Hunger and malnutrition are devastating. Blessed works in the hardest- to-reach places to address the root causes of hunger to ensure the most vulnerable grow up heathy and nourished.

Poverty

Poverty exists in every state and at every income level. But the poorest families experience higher rates of poverty and are struggling the most.

Health

People are at a high risk of direct harm from COVID-19, they are disproportionately affected by its hidden impacts. Even before the pandemic, 135 million people were already experiencing acute food insecurity.

Education

For the first time in human history, an entire generation globally have had their education disrupted. And yet, this emergency comes on top of an existing learning crisis. Even before the pandemic struck, millions were not going to school and not learning.

Climate Crisis

While climate change affects everyone, those who have contributed the least to the crisis—children, those in poverty, and future generations—are the most affected.

Emergency Response

Blessed does whatever it takes to be there for children in crisis.
